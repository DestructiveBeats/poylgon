who are we and what is polygon

A diverse selection of musical talent, visual artistry, and production, Biohazard Promotions seeks to bring all walks of talent together to create a unique experience, whether it be on a large scale with some of the most recognized DJ’s and venues in the world or in more intimate spaces with the best local talent the city has to offer. Biohazard Promotions currently markets the greatest club events and parties in NYC and beyond. Offering a party experience incomparable to anywhere else. Our infamous guest list has been used by thousands of partygoers from all over the world at some of the most famous nightspots in NYC, including Pacha NYC, Cielo, Output, The Panther Room, and Schimanski. We are extremely passionate about the underground house music scene that continues to evolve in NYC, specifically in Brooklyn, where we have collaborated with some of the best DJ’s and visual artists. Here, we have put together special events in intimate venues such as, Lot 45, Members Only, Terra Firma, and secret warehouse locations.In 2016, Biohazard Promotions launched its first NYC boat cruise series, Carried Away. The summer boat parties attracted partygoers from all over, resulting in sold out boats every time. Carried Away is best described as an immersive experience of artwork, LED light schemes, and of course, incredible deep house and techno music. Some of our past DJ’s include, Chaim, Frank & Tony, Russ Yallop, Culoe De Song, Deetron, Behrouz, Roger Sanchez, Danny Howells and more. With breathtaking views of the city and good vibes from our strong following, Carried Away remains one of the most sought after boat parties in NYC.Biohazard Promotions plans to continue to work with the best talent within the house music scene and put together a creative process that will leave those attending any of our events with an unforgettable experience.
